Nexus Systems: The Core Values

The No Hands Company (TNHC)
1. The Genesis: The "F*** NO" Moment

Nexus Systems was not born out of a desire for profit, but out of a necessity for resistance.

In an era where major platforms (like Discord) have shifted toward invasive surveillance through mandatory Face and ID scans, we reached a breaking point. When corporations demand your biometric data as the "price" of communication, the only logical response is: "F* NO."

Nexus was founded to give people the digital environment they actually deserve—one that is owned by the users, not a corporation. It is a rebellion against the "Enshittification" of the modern web.
2. Our Mission

To build a fully interconnected, sovereign digital ecosystem where privacy is the default, and the "cost of entry" is zero. We provide the tools for people to communicate, host, and deploy their digital lives without handing over their identity or their wallet.
3. The Pillars of Nexus Systems

Based on our development philosophy and the 80+ interconnected systems within the TNHC ecosystem, we operate by these three non-negotiable pillars:
I. Privacy First (Identity Sovereignty)

    No ID, No Scans: We believe your identity belongs to you. Nexus will never require biometric data or government-issued ID to function.

    Anti-Surveillance: Our systems, from communication (Nexus) to hosting (Nexus Host), are built to prevent tracking and data harvesting.

    Decentralized Power: By removing the corporate middleman, we remove the "all-seeing eye" that monitors user interactions.

II. Radical Accessibility (Always Free, No Paywalls)

    0% In-App Purchases: There are no "premium" tiers or locked features. If it’s in the system, it’s free for everyone.

    Zero-Cost Infrastructure: We solve the "pay-to-play" barrier of the cloud. Through Nexus Host and Nexus Deploy, we provide the tools to run your own services without expensive monthly subscriptions.

    Donation-Driven: We only accept money through voluntary donations. 100% of these funds are cycled back into the development and maintenance of the ecosystem.

III. Self-Hosted & Interconnected

    User Ownership: Every application is designed to be self-hosted. You don't "rent" space from us; you own your node.

    The Nexus Cloud: Despite being decentralized, our 80+ systems (including VersaAI, NLPL, and Phantom) are interconnected through a major cloud-native architecture that allows for seamless integration.

    Developer Autonomy: We provide the same tools we use. If we built a tool to solve a deployment hurdle, we give that tool to you so you can bypass the same wall.

4. The Ecosystem in Practice

The repos at The No Hands Company represent a holistic solution to digital independence:

    Nexus: The core communication platform. Privacy-focused, community-owned.

    Nexus Hosting & Deploy: The infrastructure layer. If you can’t host it for free, you don’t truly own it.

    VersaAI & NLPL: Production-grade AI and programming tools designed to work within the privacy-first ecosystem.

    The 80+ Systems: From project management (DevTrack) to hardware analysis (Whatcaniplay), every tool is a brick in a wall built to keep corporate overreach out.

5. Our Promise

Nexus Systems is, and will always be:

    Self-hosted: You have the keys.

    Free FOREVER: No subscriptions, no "freemium" traps.

    Privacy-focused: Your data is none of our business.
