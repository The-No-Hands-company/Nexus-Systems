INTENDED USAGE:
---------------
This toolkit is designed to be included in ANY C++ project to provide
comprehensive debugging, profiling, error tracking, and development
support across all phases of development from prototyping to production.

Each utility can be:
- Enabled/disabled at compile time
- Configured at runtime
- Used independently or as part of the suite
- Extended with custom implementations

PHILOSOPHY:
-----------
"Create the entire development infrastructure BEFORE starting your
actual C++ project to maximize productivity and minimize debugging time."

This toolkit embodies that philosophy by providing ready-made solutions
for virtually every diagnostic and development challenge you might face.
