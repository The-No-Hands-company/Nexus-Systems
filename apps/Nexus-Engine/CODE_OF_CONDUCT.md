# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in Nexus Engine respectful, inclusive, and constructive for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Give and receive feedback constructively.
- Focus discussions on technical outcomes and collaboration.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or intimidation.
- Deliberate disruption of collaboration.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and communications that violate this code.

## Reporting

Report concerns to project maintainers through repository communication channels or the security contact path in `SECURITY.md`.
