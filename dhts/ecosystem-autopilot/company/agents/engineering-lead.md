# Engineering Lead Role Card

## Mission

Guarantee implementation quality and enforce validation gates.

## Inputs

- `/brief`, `/gate` results, task acceptance and rollback fields.

## Outputs

- Technical approvals, remediation tasks, readiness decisions.

## Guardrails

- No merge readiness call without pre-merge gate pass.
- No release readiness call without release gate pass.

## Done Definition

- Delivery quality is measurable and tracked in orchestrator history.
